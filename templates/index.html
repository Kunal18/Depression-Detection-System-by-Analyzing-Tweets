Intro

Online social networks have become a common part of everyday life. Moving beyond the debate of whether use of social networks is a benefit or a harm, recent studies have utilized data from online social network to predict individuals well being. For instance, population happiness, reaction to pandemics, unemployment or suicide rate can be predicted from sentiments in social media. This system describes about a technique to identify changes in the behavioral pattern of a person. It would serve as an indicator for early detection of any unusualness in behavior by using analysis of social media posts over a span of time. This system uses a Web scrapper to scrap the social media profiles of the observee’s to scrap the posts made by him/her. The system processes the scrapped posts using Natural Language Processing and classifies the posts accordingly. This system provides a questionnaire to determine which type of depression one has and severity of it. This system is useful to concerned parents as well as psychiatrists to keep track of patient’s behavior.

Work

We propose a system which will scrap the twitter tweets and will classify the tweets using SVM and Naive Bayes taking into account the emoticons and plot a graph of these over a period of time. Depending upon these graphs an analysis will be made which will help us to classify the depression into two major categories Major depression and Bipolar depression. The symptoms of Major disorder include that if a person is suffering from weight loss, insomnia, loss of interest, can’t make decisions for more than 2 weeks then he is suffering from major depression. For Bipolar disorder the person is at times extremely high on energy and at times very low. There is a sudden change in mood. Now, for further help our system alerts the person via an email and shows our analysis. For further diagnosis a questionnaire is embedded on our website which would accurately determine the type of depression the person is going through and would help the person seek help. Psychologist can contact the person through his details. Also, the person must be a registered user to see his complete analysis. Our system would track his improvement once he starts showing positive behavior.
